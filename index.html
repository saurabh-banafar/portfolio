About Me

My journey into tech started with a simple curiosity—how do apps work, how is data used so intelligently, and what powers the digital world around us? That curiosity quickly grew into a passion. After a brief period of distraction, I refocused and enrolled in the BS in Data Science program at IIT Madras. Before that, I had already explored web development, which gave me hands-on experience in building user-facing applications and understanding the web's core technologies.

I'm currently in the learning phase, diving deep into data science, statistics, and machine learning. My long-term goal is to work at the intersection of AI, data, and real-world problem-solving, creating impactful, intelligent systems. I'm excited about the journey ahead and committed to growing into a skilled data scientist and AI innovator.
